Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Booking tickets is easy! Simply browse events on our platform, select your preferred event, choose ticket type and quantity, then proceed to checkout. You'll receive your QR-coded e-tickets via email immediately after successful payment.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), local bank cards, mobile payments, and online banking transfers. All transactions are secure and PCI DSS compliant.

Yes, tickets are refundable up to 48 hours before the event start time. Processing fees may apply. For events that are cancelled or postponed, full refunds are automatically processed within 7-10 business days.

Simply show your QR code (digital or printed) at the event entrance. Our staff will scan it for quick validation. Make sure to bring a valid photo ID that matches the name on your booking.
