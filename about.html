Our Story

Founded in 2020, EventHub was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make discovering and attending events as easy and enjoyable as possible for everyone in Sri Lanka.

What started as a small initiative to help local event organizers reach their audiences has grown into Sri Lanka's premier event ticketing platform. We recognized the gap between amazing events happening across the country and people who wanted to attend them.

Today, we're proud to serve over 15,000 customers, 100+ event organizers, and have facilitated the sale of more than 25,000 tickets across diverse categories including music, sports, arts, business, and cultural events.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving the future of event experiences in Sri Lanka

Our Mission

To democratize event discovery and attendance by providing a seamless, secure, and user-friendly platform that connects event organizers with their ideal audiences while ensuring unforgettable experiences for every participant.

  • Simplify event discovery and booking
  • Empower local event organizers
  • Create memorable experiences
  • Build community connections

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's leading digital events ecosystem, fostering a vibrant cultural and social landscape where every event, from intimate gatherings to large-scale festivals, finds its perfect audience and creates lasting impact.

  • Leading events platform in Sri Lanka
  • Innovative technology solutions
  • Sustainable event ecosystem
  • Cultural enrichment catalyst
